Frequently Asked Questions

CodePlay is designed for children aged 6-14. We offer different courses tailored to specific age groups and skill levels, from complete beginners to more advanced coders.

No prior coding experience is required! Our courses are designed for beginners and gradually build up to more complex concepts. We start with the basics and make learning fun and accessible.

All you need is a computer or tablet with internet access. Most of our courses work on both Windows and Mac computers. Some advanced courses may require specific software, but we'll guide you through setup.

We offer flexible subscription plans starting at $9.99/month for individual accounts. We also have family plans and annual subscriptions at discounted rates. Check our pricing page for full details.
